Common Questions
Frequently Asked Questions
Can you list my spa without my permission?
We list public business information (name, address, phone, website) which is publicly available — the same way Google Maps or Yelp do. However, to earn the GlowRanked Verified™ badge and access premium features, you need to apply and pass our verification process. You can also claim your listing at any time to manage and update it.
How long does verification take?
Our AI verification engine typically completes within 24–72 hours. For complex cases or additional credential checks, it may take up to 5 business days. You'll be notified at every step.
What happens if we don't pass verification?
We'll let you know exactly what flagged and give you the opportunity to resolve it. Common issues include expired licenses or incomplete documentation — most are resolved quickly. You can still have a basic listing while verification is pending.
Is there a contract or lock-in period?
No contracts, no lock-ins. All plans are month-to-month. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time. Annual plans are pre-paid and non-refundable, but save you 25%.
What makes GlowRanked different from Yelp or RealSelf?
GlowRanked is exclusively focused on med spas and aesthetic clinics — not restaurants, plumbers, or general doctors. Our verification is active (we check credentials ourselves) not passive (we just let anyone pay to list). And our audience is specifically searching for aesthetic treatments, making every lead far more qualified than general platforms.
How do you handle my reviews?
We import your existing verified reviews from Google and other platforms, run them through our AI authenticity engine, and only display reviews that pass our legitimacy checks. You can also collect new reviews directly through GlowRanked — all authenticated and tied to real client visits.
