Section 02

4-Week Launch Plan

Revenue goal: close 3–5 paying charters in Week 1. Build consumer traffic in Weeks 2–4 to demonstrate ROI and close upsells.

Week 1 — Revenue Now
Outbound charter outreach to all 22 listed spas
Call every spa currently in the directory. Script: takeaway close — "we can only verify one spa per zip code, yours is on the shortlist." Verification is free. Send the claim link during the call. Goal: 5 calls per day, 3 paid upgrades by Friday.
↑ Revenue Target: $591–$2,985 MRR
Week 1–2 — SEO Foundation
Publish city landing pages + optimize for local search
Create individual pages for each city: /rancho-cucamonga, /chino-hills, /riverside, etc. Each page targets "med spa [city] CA" with verified listings, treatments, and FAQ schema. Submit sitemap to Google Search Console.
Target: Top 5 Google for "med spa [city] CA" in 60–90 days
Week 2 — Social Proof Launch
Instagram + TikTok: "Which IE med spas are actually verified?"
Create content around the trust gap: "We checked 50 IE med spas. Here's what we found." Short-form video, carousel posts, and "verified vs unverified" side-by-side content. Tag listed spas — they'll reshare, giving free reach.
Platform: Instagram Reels, TikTok, Facebook Groups
Week 3 — Consumer Traffic
Launch "Best Med Spas in [City]" content for each city
Blog-style city guides targeting consumer search intent. "Best med spas in Rancho Cucamonga 2026" — pulls GlowRanked to page 1 for high-intent searches. Each guide links to the directory and verified listings.
Compounding: organic traffic drives consumer trust → spa demand
Week 4 — Upsell + Expand
Follow up with verified spas — upgrade pitch
Once spas see their verified listing live, call back with the upgrade offer. Show them search traffic data. Offer Featured ($397) or Premier ($597) for the city lock. Expand outreach to San Bernardino, Victorville, Murrieta.
Goal: 2–3 upgrades per verified Charter spa
